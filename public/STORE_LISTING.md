🇬🇧 English Listing

Title (Max 45 chars):
Quick Translator

Short Summary (Max 132 chars):
Translate selected text instantly — right on the page. Supports 24 languages, keeps formatting, and works without reloading.

Detailed Description (Max 16,000 chars):
Quick Translator is a blazing-fast, lightweight extension designed to make reading foreign languages on the web seamless. Stop copy-pasting text into new tabs — translate exactly what you need, right where you are reading it.

✨ Key Features:
- In-Place Translation: Select any text on any webpage and click the floating icon to translate it instantly. The translated text replaces the original seamlessly.
- Smart Formatting: We preserve the original HTML structure! Paragraphs, lists, and line breaks stay exactly as they were, so the page layout doesn't break.
- Undo in One Click: Need to see the original text again? Just click the "Undo" button on the notification bar that appears at the bottom of the screen.
- 24 Languages Supported: Translate to and from English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Russian, Arabic, and many more.
- Customizable Interface: Choose your preferred language for the extension's popup interface.

🚀 How to use:
1. Select any text on a webpage.
2. Click the small "Translate" button that pops up near your cursor.
3. Read the translation right on the page!

🔒 Privacy First:
Quick Translator respects your privacy. We do not track your browsing history, and we do not store your translated texts. The text you select is sent directly and securely to the translation API and nowhere else.

🚀 Как использовать:
1. Выделите любой текст на сайте.
2. Нажмите на маленькую кнопку перевода рядом с курсором.
3. Читайте перевод прямо на странице!

🔒 Конфиденциальность:
Быстрый Переводчик уважает вашу приватность. Мы не отслеживаем историю браузера и не сохраняем переведенные тексты. Выделенный вами текст отправляется напрямую по защищенному каналу к API переводчика и больше никуда.

Privacy Policy for Quick Translator

Last updated: July 2026

Quick Translator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Data Collection and Usage
- Text Translation: When you select text and click the translate button, that specific selected text is sent securely to the translation API (Google Translate) solely for the purpose of returning a translation. We do not intercept, store, or log this text on any external servers owned by us.
- Local Storage: We use Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local) to save your language preferences (e.g., your chosen target language and UI language). This data never leaves your device.

2. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information or browsing habits to outside parties. The selected text is only shared with the translation provider necessary to perform the core function of the extension.

3. Analytics and Tracking
Quick Translator does not use any third-party analytics trackers, and we do not track your browsing history or behavior across websites.

4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
